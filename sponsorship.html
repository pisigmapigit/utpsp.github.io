Getting Involved

Thank you to our corporate supporters! By supporting Pi Sigma Pi, you will enable us to reach out to a pool of more than 1000 minority engineering students at The University of Texas at Austin. While enrolled in rigorous majors, members strive to create a family among Pi-NSBE-SHPE to promote academic success and challenge grade school students to pursue higher education. We are proud of the accomplishments we have made as individuals and as a group, and we look forward to achieving even greater success in the year to come.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at utexas.pisigmapi@gmail.com.
